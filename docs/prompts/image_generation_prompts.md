# 🖼️ Image Generation Prompts - FieldLaunch Assets

These prompts are designed for an external, high-end AI image generator (e.g., Midjourney, DALL-E 3) and must be used to create all visual assets for the site templates.

**Mandatory Style:** Cinematic, Architectural Photography, High Dynamic Range (HDR), Premium, Master Craftsman Aesthetic. **NEVER** use generic stock photos.

---

## 🏠 Roofing Niche Prompts

**General Aesthetic:** Focus on structural integrity, craftsmanship, longevity, and premium materials. The mood should be reliable and sophisticated.

### 1. Service: Re-Roofing & Replacements
*   **Concept:** The perfect finished roof line.
*   **Prompt:** *Architectural photo, drone view of a brand new, dark charcoal shingle roof under a blue sky. Focus on the crisp, clean lines, uniform color, and perfect ridge caps. Sunlight reflection off the clean surface. Cinematic depth of field, high dynamic range. Cinematic, luxury home builder magazine.*

### 2. Service: Insurance Claims
*   **Concept:** The professional assessment process.
*   **Prompt:** *A focused, close-up shot of a roofer's gloved hands using a specialized, high-resolution camera to document specific damage (e.g., a curled shingle edge). The background is partially obscured by clouds/mist. Emphasize precision and expert documentation. Cinematic, gritty realism, forensic photography style.*

### 3. Service: Maintenance & Repairs
*   **Concept:** The careful, targeted repair.
*   **Prompt:** *Medium shot of a roofer carefully applying new, seamless sealant around a flashing penetration. Focus on the detail of the material application and the roofer's focused, professional posture. The scene should be bright and well-lit, emphasizing the high-quality, localized repair. Master craftsman aesthetic.*

### 4. Service: Storm Damage Claims
*   **Concept:** The immediate response to damage.
*   **Prompt:** *Dramatic, wide-angle shot of a roof under the aftermath of a storm. Focus on the contrast between the pristine, intact sections and a defined, damaged area. Include visible tarps or a specialized assessment drone. The atmosphere should be one of urgent, professional problem-solving, not panic. Moody, cinematic, high contrast.*

### 5. Service: Flexible Financing
*   **Concept:** The barrier to entry being removed.
*   **Prompt:** *A clean, stylized flat-lay graphic shot: a hand placing a "0 Down" graphic element onto a detailed blueprint of a home's roof structure. The lighting should be bright and optimistic. Minimalist, corporate design aesthetic, emphasizing ease and accessibility.*

### 6. Service: Roofing, Siding & Gutters
*   **Concept:** The harmonious exterior upgrade.
*   **Prompt:** *A picturesque, wide-angle view of a newly completed, multi-material exterior. The shot must show the seamless transition between new siding (e.g., fiber cement), immaculate gutters, and the dark shingles. The goal is to show one cohesive, unified architectural package. Golden hour lighting, hyper-realistic.*

## 🌡️ HVAC Niche Prompts

**General Aesthetic:** Focus on precision, efficiency, comfort, and reliable performance. The style should be clean, modern, and technically advanced.

### 1. Service: AC Installation & Replacement
*   **Concept:** The perfect blend of tech and comfort.
*   **Prompt:** *Interior shot of a modern living room bathed in natural light, with a newly installed, sleek, high-efficiency unit subtly visible. The focus should be on the ambient comfort and the cool, clean feel of the space, not just the machine. Minimalist, bright, aspirational, and technically perfect. Photographed at eye-level.*

### 2. Service: Heating Installation & Replacement
*   **Concept:** The cozy, safe environment.
*   **Prompt:** *Interior shot during winter. A family gathered in a warm, comfortable room. The visible furnace/heat pump unit should be modern and efficient-looking. Focus on the feeling of warmth, safety, and consistent heat. Soft, golden, inviting lighting. High contrast, cinematic winter scene.*

### 3. Service: AC Repair
*   **Concept:** The diagnostic expertise in action.
*   **Prompt:** *Close-up, professional shot of a NATE-certified technician's hands using a specialized diagnostic tool (like a manifold gauge or infrared camera) on an HVAC unit. Focus on the hands, the tools, and the focused concentration of the professional. The background should be slightly blurred (bokeh) to keep focus on the expert diagnosis. Technical realism.*

### 4. Service: Heating Repair
*   **Concept:** The core system fix.
*   **Prompt:** *A focused shot inside the mechanics of a furnace, highlighting a specific, cleanly repaired part (e.g., a newly cleaned burner assembly). The image should convey mechanical complexity and precision. Deep contrast between the metal parts and the clean work. Industrial aesthetic, cinematic quality, highly detailed.*

### 5. Service: Preventive Maintenance
*   **Concept:** The preventative checkup.
*   **Prompt:** *A clean shot of an HVAC unit with a small, visible maintenance checklist pinned to it. The scene is bright and organized, symbolizing preparedness. Include a small, premium "Maintenance Complete" sticker. The feeling should be proactive care and peace of mind. Bright, clean, professional.*

### 6. Service: 24/7 Emergency Service
*   **Concept:** Reliability under pressure.
*   **Prompt:** *A shot taken at dusk or night, showing a service vehicle (clean, branded, reliable looking) parked outside a home. A worker is visible, working under the glow of a portable work light. The focus is on immediate, reliable response, emphasizing that help is always available. Cinematic, high drama lighting.*
